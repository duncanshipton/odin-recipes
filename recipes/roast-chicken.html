Roast Chicken

Description

Roasting a chicken is one of my favorite meals, it can feed a few people, can be paired with a lot of other dishes and tastes delicious, plus it's super easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tarragon

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven

    365 Degrees Farenheit

    2. Prep Chicken

    Place Chicken in a pyrex dish or other oven-safe cookware.

    3. Add spices

    Coat in olive oil and add a light rub of salt and pepper finish with a heavy sprinkling of Tarragon

    4. Cook Chicken

    Place in oven for 1 1/2 hours. Depending on the size and weight of the chicken this can be a little more, but is usually not less, to check if the chicken is done you can use a thermometer or cut into the thickest part of the breast or thigh to make sure there is no uncooked meat in there.

    5. Voila!

    You have amazing roast chicken for dinner. :)

    Bonus!

    You will notice that in the bottom of your pyrex dish there is now a bunch of oil and delicious chickeny smelling liquid. Save them! These drippings are perfect for adding to the stock if you want to make chicken soup.
